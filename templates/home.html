ABOUT

"Identifying Fake News with Machine Learning Algorithm"

Fake news has been around for a certain time and then it has always been a problem but not many people thought of it as big of a problem. The main reason why we made this program is because people depend on news to keep informed and they are terrible at differentiating real and fake news.

We have seen that the damage from fake news is disastrous, and we want to provide a workable solution to help solve fake news detection problem. We think if people are not able to detect it themselves, then machines might do the job for us.

So thats why we decided to create this "Fake News Detection App"

We hope that by making this app, we can help many people to identify fake news at its detection stage, and by doing so we hope that people will realize how disastrous fake news is, and will take fake news seriously.

Fake News Detection App!

Just copy the article into the textbox and let the Fake News Maching Learning Algorithm check it for you!

How to use:

  • Step 1: Find the article on the internet which you want to check for
  • Step 2: Copy whole article's content into the textbox
  • Step 3: Click the "Check" button to check
  • Step 4: And you will get your result!

How It Works:

We are using Python as our core programming langguage, and with the help of Flask to communicate with the HTML webpage, we deliver our app in a user-friendly and responsive web application.

We create our program with a machine learning algorithm packages from Scikit-learn. The packages that we are using can be found here. We train our machine to detect fake news of US political news with only US political news dataset. We are using text-preprocessing, to process raw news, so we can get text calssification for the machine learning algorithm to analyze. Then our machine will give in return the probability score which indicates the precentage that the news is likely to be real.

Dataset

We trained our machine with 3 datasets that we acquired from the internet. These datasets are available online and are widely used in some recent machine learning project.

  • Fake_or_Real_News Dataset
  • LIAR Dataset
  • Kaggle Dataset

Text Preprocessing

Text cleaning is important for our machine to classify the news keywords. The machine process the article by:

  • making it lowercase
  • removing punctuation and stopwords
  • lemmatization: transforming words into its simplest form

Feature Extraction

Then we extract features from the cleaned article using Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency. One of widely used features extraction algorithm used in machine learning.

Classification

We then classify the extracted features into Support Vector Classifier.
